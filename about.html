About US

Go & Save
All-in-one app for E-Ambulance Service Providers.

Go & Save Ambulance App serve the Citizen of Pakistan. In which users can quickly and simply book an ambulance. The user must choose the hospital, pick-up location, and ambulance size. In an emergency, the user only needs to choose the pick-up location and destination; the system will then reserve the closest ambulance and hospital on their behalf. Once a booking is made, an alert is sent to the ambulance operator to confirm the booking. The pick-up and drop-off locations are visible to the ambulance driver on Google Maps. The users will be given the driver's contact information. The booking history is also accessible to hospitals. In situations of medical emergency, this ambulance booking app will save lives in this way

Mission

Simplify the process for calling ambulances in an emergency.

Vission

Empower and equip the millions to save the lives of those they love..
